What is a LandGlyder?

LandGlyder is going to change the way you travel. It's a two-wheeled self-balancing scooter. Just like a Segway without the handlebars which makes it more portable. The LandGlyder is controlled by balance, motors and pressure excerted by the feet on the feet sensors. Our products are high-quality, with a superior design.

So how do you use a LandGlyder?

It’s all based on your brain! you tell it what to do. If you lean forward, then the LandGlyder goes forward. If you want to go backwards then do it!. Right left or spin, it will all be as easy as walking. The pads control everything – they consist of two pressures sensors on each side that allows the LandGlyder to respond to your movements. It only takes 5 minutes to master the LandGlyder, And once your accustomed to your new 'Glyding' ability, the worlds the limit!
